A clean, conversion-focused single-page website designed to drive bookings for 1-on-1 mentorship sessions.

---

## 1. Hero Section
- Bold headline: e.g. *"Navigate Your Tech Career With Confidence"*
- Subheading positioning Rohan as a Senior Backend Engineer with 8+ years of experience
- Primary CTA button: **"Book a 45-Minute Call — ₹1,999"**
- Clean, minimal layout with generous whitespace

## 2. About Section
- Brief bio: 8+ years in backend engineering, based in Bangalore
- Key credibility points (technologies, companies, mentees helped)
- Professional but approachable tone

## 3. What's Included Section
- Clear breakdown of what happens in a 45-minute session (e.g. career roadmap review, resume feedback, interview prep, system design guidance)
- Presented as a clean card or icon-based grid

## 4. Testimonials Section
- 2 realistic testimonials with names, roles, and short quotes
- Clean card layout with subtle styling

## 5. Pricing Section
- Single pricing card: **₹1,999 / 45-minute session**
- List of included benefits
- CTA button to book

## 6. FAQ Section
- Accordion-style expandable questions (5-6 common questions about the mentorship process, booking, refunds, etc.)

## 7. Final CTA Section
- Strong closing headline encouraging action
- Repeated "Book a 45-Minute Call" button

## Design & Layout
- **Color**: White background, dark text, one subtle accent color (indigo/blue)
- **Typography**: Modern, clean sans-serif
- **Fully mobile responsive**
- **Sticky header** with navigation links and a CTA button
- Smooth scroll between sections
